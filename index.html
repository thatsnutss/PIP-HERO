How it works

Built around explicit rules and repeatable execution. No signals, no discretion — just structure you can follow and review.

Define context

Use trend, regime, and session filters to decide when trading is allowed — before thinking about entries.

Apply mechanical rules

Entries, exits, and no-trade conditions are rule-based, reducing second-guessing and impulse decisions.

Respect risk guardrails

Designed to support drawdown limits, session boundaries, and trade-frequency discipline.

Review and repeat

Because the rules are explicit, journaling becomes objective — helping you improve with consistency.
