FAQs

Clear answers to common questions before you get started.

Are these signals or trade alerts?

No. PIP HERO does not provide signals, alerts, or trade calls.

The indicator highlights when predefined entry conditions are valid, allowing you to decide whether to execute the trade.

The automated bot executes trades automatically, but only according to a fixed ruleset that you configure in advance — without discretionary decision-making.

Who are these tools designed for?

These tools are built for traders who want structure, rules, and consistency in their execution.

They are not designed for beginners looking for signals, shortcuts, or guaranteed outcomes, and are best suited to traders willing to follow defined rules and review performance objectively.

What platforms are supported?

The indicator and automated trading bot are delivered via TradingView and operate within TradingView’s supported brokers and instruments.

Community-based products, such as the Inner Circle, are delivered via Discord.

How do I receive access after purchase?

After purchase, you’ll receive a confirmation email containing your PDF setup guide.

The guide includes access instructions, links, and configuration steps for your product.

If the email doesn’t arrive within 10 minutes, please check your spam folder before contacting support.

Do you guarantee results or profitability?

No. Trading involves risk, and outcomes depend on execution, discipline, and market conditions.

PIP HERO provides rules-based tools that define when trading conditions are valid, but does not provide investment advice, performance guarantees, or profit expectations.

Does the indicator place trades automatically?

No. The indicator is analytical only and does not place trades.

Trades are executed manually unless you are using the automated trading bot.
